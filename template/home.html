Censorship Free Blogging

NoTex is a blogging and general publishing platform, that allows you to express your thoughts absolutely freely without any compromises.

Thanks to a revolutionary technology called IPFS you are enabled to publish anything and everything without NoTex being able to block you!

Also, it does not require a login and hence an identification of your person. It protects your privacy further by not storing in any way data in a centralized cloud environment.

Instant Preview

At its core NoTex is simply an editor, that allows you to draft your content using an intuitive syntax called Markdown. If you don't know Markdown do not worry, because there is a toolbar that supports you while composing a document.

While writing, your content is displayed in a preview panel, allowing you to get a quick impression of how your final blog post or publication will look alike. This includes fancy renderings of simple or complicated mathematical formulae.

One-Click Publication

You can publish your blog posts with a single click by hitting the blue publish button, and your content will be made available to the general public (via an IPFS gateway).

If you want to, you are free – but not obligated – to provide metadata like a title, description, author(s), email(s) or keyword(s).

NoTex also supports publishing to Blogger.com, which however is not censorship resistant, and has its own terms and conditions.

Unstoppable Delivery

Once published (via an IPFS gateway) you can share your blog post with anybody on the internet via the URL address.

Depending on the IPFS gateway you have chosen to publish with, your content might get deleted or even deliberately censored – which is outside of NoTex's control!

However, by installing the IPFS desktop application and pinning your content any IPFS compatible browser will be able to access your original blog post.

Also, by enabling the IPFS Companion plugin for Chrome or add-on for Firefox, both browsers will become IPFS friendly.

Blockchain Certification

If you have an Ethereum (or Avalanche) compatible wallet like Metamask installed, then you can have your content certified on the corresponding blockchain – in exchange for a nominal (i.e. almost free) fee, directly paid to the blockchain network.

The resulting PDF certificate links to a so called non-fungible token (NFT), witnessing that it was indeed you who has published the blog post. This enables the protection of your rights on your intellectual property and even makes your blog post tradable!
